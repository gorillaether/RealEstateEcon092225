The Long-Term Story

How to Read This Chart:

On this "log" chart, the straight green line shows steady inflation. Things that grow at a constant rate are always straight lines.

Notice how the red line (housing) curves sharply upward, pulling away from the steady inflation trend. This shows an unsustainable acceleration in price.

The Path Back to Average

A 6-Year Horizon:

This shows what happens if prices fall by 3.2% annually, while the inflation trend grows by 3.2%.

Questions for Today's Homebuyer

1. Am I Buying Shelter or Speculating?

(Insight from Slide 5) For 60+ years, home prices tracked inflation. The recent price acceleration is a historical anomaly. Are you betting this anomaly will continue?

2. Am I Comfortable with Today's Premium?

(Insight from Slide 6) Prices today are roughly 45% above the historical inflation trend. What economic forces justify paying such a premium right now?

3. Is My Time Horizon Long Enough?

(Insight from Slide 7) A return to the average could take 6+ years, during which prices may be flat or declining. Can you commit to staying in the home for at least 10 years to weather a potential correction?
